======================================================================
1. PROBLEM STATEMENT
======================================================================

Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and Community Health Centres (CHCs) form the backbone of India's public healthcare system. However, most centres still operate using fragmented and largely manual processes for inventory management, attendance tracking, patient management, resource allocation and reporting.

This results in several recurring operational challenges:

• Medicine stock-outs due to delayed reporting
• Expired medicines leading to wastage
• Lack of visibility across neighbouring PHCs and CHCs
• Uneven patient distribution causing overloaded facilities
• Unavailable beds despite spare capacity nearby
• Doctor absenteeism
• Equipment failures remaining unnoticed
• Delayed response during emergencies
• No prediction of future demand
• Poor coordination between health centres and district administration
• No intelligent redistribution of available resources
• Lack of centralized decision making

Healthcare administrators are forced to react after problems occur rather than preventing them.

The objective of this project is to transform district healthcare management from reactive to proactive using Artificial Intelligence.

======================================================================
3. PRODUCT VISION
======================================================================

ArogyaNet AI is an AI-powered District Health Intelligence and Operations Platform that connects every PHC, CHC, district hospital, ambulance, administrator and emergency authority into one intelligent ecosystem.

Instead of merely displaying healthcare data, the platform continuously analyzes operational data, predicts future demand, recommends actions, coordinates multiple departments and generates real-time alerts.

The platform should become the AI Operating System for an entire district's public healthcare infrastructure.

13. SUCCESS CRITERIA
======================================================================

The project is successful if it demonstrates an end-to-end workflow where:

1. PHCs/CHCs continuously submit operational data.
2. AI analyzes the incoming data in real time.
3. AI predicts shortages, demand, and operational risks.
4. The system generates intelligent recommendations.
5. Officials receive actionable alerts.
6. The district dashboard updates dynamically.
7. Healthcare resources can be proactively redistributed before shortages occur.

The ultimate vision is to transform district healthcare from a reactive system into an intelligent, predictive, AI-powered public health operations platform capable of improving efficiency, reducing wastage, preventing shortages, and enabling faster, data-driven decisions across the entire healthcare ecosystem.
